Mixins

What you might need when developing widgets of your own.

  • FhTableDefinitionMixin

    Provide uniform way of storing and getting instance of FhTableDefinition through html markup.

    Instance of TableDefinition will be stored as $scope.params, aleviating the need for every widgets in hierarchical structure to ask for that same instance.
