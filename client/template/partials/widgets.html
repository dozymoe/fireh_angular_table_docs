Widgets

All packaged widgets are set as attribute directives.

The widgets don't have to be laid in an hierarchical structure. Your <table> can be located in the middle of the page while your filter and sorting widget in the sidebar or somewhere else. Those scattered widgets will communicate by means of instance of FhTableDefinition.

Demo

See demo of all widgets in action.

Notice that all helper widgets are outside of the scope of FhTable, they are not inside the table html element, but somehow they managed to communicate with the table.
