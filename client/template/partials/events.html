Events

Interact with the whole framework, get in touch with its event-hub.

There are two types of interactions:

  • passive interaction, where you just reacted when an event occured.
  • overpowered interaction, where you can alter the behaviour of widgets with middlewares.

There are two types of events:

  • action event, where changes happens and ajax calls were made.
  • display event, after the fact event, you get to know if the previous action where successful or failed, and update the user interface accordingly.
